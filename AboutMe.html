The 7 Tenets of The Satanic Temple

I. One shoud strive to act with compassion and empathy towards all creatures in accordance with reason. II. The Struggle for justice is an ongoing and necessary persuit that should prevail over laws and institutions. III. One's body is inviolable, subject to one's own will alone. IV. The freedoms of others should to be respected, including the freedom to offend. To willingly and unjustlyencroach upon the freedoms of another is to forgo one's own. V. Beliefs should conform to one's best scientific understanding of the world. One should take care to never distort scientific facts to fit one's beliefs. VI. People are fallible. If one person makes a mistake, one should do one's best to rectify it and resolve any harm that may have been caused. VII. Every tenet is a guiding principle designed to inspire nobility in action and thought. The spirit of compassion, wisdom, and justice should always prevail over the written or spoken word.

About Me

From what I have designed this site to be, to what it offers in relation to who I am and what it is that I offer… I am not the easiest of people to know. To some that I know; I am too dark, too intense, too clingy, too trusting, too gullible, too cynical, too bitter. At all times I wear multiple masks and it sometimes becomes hard to know who I am at times. But this is the point to why I offer what I do - why I do what I do.

Come with me into insanity

My name is Erin Nyght and I have DID (Dissociative Identity Disorder) or Multiple Personality Disorder that fully manifested later in life: and after my nervous system started to relax from a constant “Survival Mode” is when the fracture of all my identities became apparent. I need to make it clear that I am the remaining personality from the old system of alternates left after a shamanic healing journey integrated my brothers and sisters of that system back into the host personality. But the host personality at the time thought it best to leave and vanish instead of finishing her healing journey. PTSD is a bitch. When she left, I was the only personality to host the body. To those who knew her: her name was Savanna Tres. But to me, she will always be the period of transition in a three fold journey - out of nothingness.

Or a Chrysalis of nightmares - if ye like…

As for me: I was the original host to this body long ago. My mind was already fragile and defenseless: I was prone to flights of fantasy and dreaming boldly. I loved deeply. I was raised Jodu Shin-Shu Buddhist but left the temple at 13. After leaving the temple I found a book on shamanism that I felt drawn to. My problems started when I started practicing the Journey methods: and when I started experimenting with partitioning & compartmentalizing my mind so the body can remain functioning in life while I send my consciousness elsewhere.

Because whilst the body was able to function on autopilot with the consciousness out on a journey: there wasn't enough of me inhabiting the body, if the body did anything that needed full consciousness there: which it did whilst I was away.

Rule 1 of Shamanic Journeying: When in a meditative trance the body and mind need to be in a safe location so the consciousness may return to the body safely. The body was safe - the mind was not. With that I could not reenter the body - my body. I was exiled in the subconsciousness for decades whilst the host that took over my body started to degrade. Remember: the consciousness that inhabited the body was only enough to keep the body running on autopilot. And that autopilot fractured in a nightmare that triggered perpetual survival mode.

Do I know what happened to the body over those lost 25 years I was in exile - no. Maybe fragments of impressions and memory here and there, but that's all. As a restored host personality whose only been back out since late Aug ‘24, I've been more focused on putting the Autopilot personality to bed whilst regaining Sovereignty, Self Empowerment and moving on from the Chrysalis of Nightmares that does haunt my dreams.

As it is now, I practice Lilithian Satanism, I study the Qliphoth, I read the Tarot as a way to gaze heavily into the abyss of my own psyche: to understand and to make peace with my rage, my demons and all the ways I wish to scream at the world. I sit with the raging inferno of my anger not to diminish the flames but to be comfortable with the heat. But in regards to having DID; I am an integrated woman, and the only one in my head. But I also acknowledge that if the cards aren't played right: another fracture is only a matter of time.

So why do I offer what I do? Because I know the pain of being lost in the abyss of one's own mind. I know the terror of being trapped
